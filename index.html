About

With a background in geography and Asian studies, Katrina enjoys creating maps, collecting data, and spreading geographical knowledge. Currently, she works with the National Park Service to design and create interactive web maps.
From navigating with topographic maps in the mountains of Colorado to analyzing oil palm plantation imagery around Southeast Asia to teaching the Indonesian provincial government how to collect spatial data, Katrina has worked on a range of geospatial projects. Katrina aims to make the world more geograpically and map literate. She lives for making the world a mappier place.
